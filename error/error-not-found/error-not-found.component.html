A 404 error indicates that the requested web page or resource could not be found on the server. It typically occurs due to a broken or non-existent URL, removed or relocated content, or mistyped links. Double-check the URL and try again. If the issue persists, the resource may have been permanently deleted or moved, and you should contact the website owner or server administrator for further assistance.
